Your Goals

Pay Yourself First

Treat savings like a bill. Transfer money to your goal the day you get paid, before spending.

Start Small

Even $25/week adds up to $1,300/year. Consistency beats big one-time deposits.

Your Debts
Pay minimums on everything, throw extra money at the smallest balance. Quick wins build momentum.

Pick a Strategy

Snowball gives quick wins for motivation. Avalanche saves more money. Pick the one you'll stick with.

Extra Payments

Avoid New Debt

While paying off debt, freeze credit card spending. Use cash or debit to avoid backsliding.
